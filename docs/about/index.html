What is Digital Design and Emergent Media?

"Digital design forces you to think outside of the box and think about not only what's happening now, but what is possible for the future." -Quiana W.

The way in which the world conveys and consumes information is rapidly changing. Digitial design and emergent media characterizes the intentional creation of a diverse set of constantly evolving innovative and interactive technologies to communicate ideas and information. It speaks to the way we experience images, music, video, apps, the internet and other immersive technologies. Integration, collaboration and adaptability are the hallmarks this cutting-edge, interactive participatory media.
